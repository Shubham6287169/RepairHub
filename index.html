FAQ's

Q. What types of damages does Repairhub repair?

Repairhub repairs various types of damages including cracked screens, motherboard issues, water damage, battery replacement, and more.

The duration of smartphone repair depends on the type and severity of the damage. However, Repairhub ensures that the repair process is completed as soon as possible and the device is delivered to the customer within the shortest possible time.

Q. Can I book a mobile repair service online?

Yes, You can book a mobile repair service online through Repairhub's website or mobile app.

Q. Does Repairhub use genuine parts for its repairs?

Yes, Repairhub uses genuine parts for its repairs. In case genuine parts are not available, Repairhub uses high-quality OEM parts.

Q. Can Repairhub repair all brands of smartphones?

Yes, Repairhub can repair all popular brands of smartphones including Samsung, Apple, Google, Oneplus, Vivo, Oppo, Mi, Nokia, and more.

Q. Is it safe to hand over my device to Repaihub for repair?

Yes, it is completely safe to hand over your device to Repairhub for repair. Repairhub ensures complete data privacy and security.

Q. How does Doorstep Mobile Repair work?

Upon booking our Doorstep Mobile Repair service, our esteemed customer care team will reach out to you to determine your preferred timing slot for our technician's visit. The technician will perform a comprehensive diagnosis of your mobile device and provide on-the-spot repairs at your doorstep. If the issue is deemed major, our technician will transport your device to an authorized center for prompt resolution.

Q. Does Repairhub provide a warranty for its repairs?

Yes, Repairhub provides a warranty on its repairs. The warranty period varies depending on the type of repair and the brand of the device.

Q. How can I track the status of my repair?

You can track the status of your repair online by logging into your Repairhub account.

Q. How do I pay for the repair service?

You can pay for the repair service online through Repairhub's website or mobile app using various payment methods including debit/credit cards, net banking, and UPI.
